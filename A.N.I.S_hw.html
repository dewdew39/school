Al Noor International School of Bahrain

Al Noor International School is an international school in Bahrain with three curriculums. It is primarily for Arabs and non-Arabs alike, powered and controlled by Cambridge.

These 3 curriculums are as follows:

  • British Curriculum: For non-Bahrainis
  • Bahrain Curriculum: Mainly for Bahrainis
  • CBSE Curriculum: For Indians alike

The languages taught in these 3 curriculums are:

  1. British curriculum: English and Arabic or French
  2. Arabic curriculum: English and Arabic
  3. CBSE curriculum: English and Arabic, French or Hindi
